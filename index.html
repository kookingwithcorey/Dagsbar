About DAGS Bar

DAGS Bar is a passion-project bourbon lounge. It’s built around the idea of a cozy, whiskey-only space with a deep bourbon list, thoughtful cocktails, and the feel of a private whiskey library.

This site is a concept only — not an operating business — but everything here is ready for the day the doors actually open.

  • Whiskey-only program with heavy bourbon focus
  • Classic, whiskey-forward cocktails
  • Curated flights for every level of enthusiast
  • Imagined in Portsmouth, New Hampshire

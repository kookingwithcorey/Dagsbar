About

DAGS Bar is a private, whiskey-only concept bourbon lounge located in Portsmouth, New Hampshire. Focused on rare expressions, allocated barrels, small-batch distillers, and ultra-premium American whiskey. Not a public bar — an atmosphere built around craftsmanship, conversation, and the shared love of whiskey.

Whiskey Menu

Explore a curated selection of rare bourbons, ryes, American whiskey, scotch, and tequila. Bottles range from private single barrels to unicorn releases and ultra-aged expressions.

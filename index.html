About DAGS

DAGS lives somewhere between a speakeasy, a whiskey museum, and a living room. It’s a concept-first lounge built around the collection itself: shelves of bourbon and rye, comfortable leather seating, and walls that tell stories.

The Concept

  • • Whiskey-only program with a heavy bourbon focus
  • • Single barrel, vintage, and allocated bottles on rotation
  • • Flights that walk guests from approachable to wild
  • • Classic cocktails built around the bottle list
  • • Intentionally small, cozy, and conversation-first

The Room

  • • Leather couches, vintage rugs, and aged wood paneling
  • • Bottle room & cellar lined with shelves of whiskey
  • • Art, records, and memorabilia layered into the walls
  • • A whiskey sanctuary forged from “Live Free or Die” spirit—unpretentious, warm, and welcoming.

Visit (Concept)

DAGS is not an open business (yet). Think of this as a living concept deck for a future whiskey lounge, a home bar turned full experience, and a place to collect ideas until the right space appears.

For now, DAGS exists in photos, bottles, and late-night pours with friends. If this ever turns into a real public lounge in Portsmouth, NH, this site will be the first place to announce it.
